What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the field of computer science focused on building systems that can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. Below are four major areas that power many of the AI applications we use today.

Area 1

Machine Learning (ML)

Machine Learning is about teaching computers to learn patterns from data.

  • Predictive modeling
  • Recommendations
  • Reinforcement learning
Area 2

Natural Language Processing (NLP)

NLP enables computers to work with human language.

  • Chatbots
  • Machine translation
  • Sentiment analysis
Area 3

Computer Vision

Computer Vision allows machines to interpret visual data.

  • Object detection
  • Facial recognition
  • Medical imaging
Area 4

Robotics

Robotics combines AI with physical machines.

  • Manufacturing robots
  • Warehouse robots
  • Autonomous drones
